Mobile Robot

Design and manufacturing lab final project. Learned to machine components using lathe, mill, and CNC. Gained experience in the fundamentals of design process, dimensional tolerancing, and manufacruing.

Accomplishments

Designed and manufactured a robot that can collect, sort, and dispense tennis and golf balls.

Learned and excuted the design process. Sketched out the concepts; selected optimal subsystem designs using decision matrices; created models in SolidWorks; cardboard prototype to validate the concept; machined the wheel hubs using manual lathe and mill; bent and welded sheetmetal parts; ultimately assemble and test the robot.

Created a comprehensive design report that documented the entire process of this project.
