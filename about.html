my journey

I'm Lezhou (pronounced Le-JOE) and I received my M.Eng degree in Mechanical Engineering with a concentration in Product Design from Berkeley. I hold a B.S. degree in the same discipline from UF with a minor in Mathematics. I am currently doing an engineering rotational program across product development teams at Rivian.

I enjoy learning the physics of objects and understanding how a complicated machine is built from the simplest systems. I also like to figure out how they can be improved, and use my knowledge to create new solutions to the challenging problems.

I hope to bring my skills to a collaborative team and improve people's lives by identifying opportunity gaps and creating innovative products.
